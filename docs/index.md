# Dr Rashidatu Abdulazeez

Evolutionary geneticist and ecotoxicologist investigating how environmental toxins shape population dynamics, evolutionary processes, and disease aetiology, using Drosophila as a model.

Affiliations  
- Institute of Ecology and Evolution, School of Biological Sciences, University of Edinburgh, UK  
- Department of Zoology, Faculty of Life Sciences, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria

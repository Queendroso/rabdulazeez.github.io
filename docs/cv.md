## Profile

I am a host‑pathogen evolutionary geneticist and ecotoxicologist. I ask how chronic environmental exposures like heavy metals, food additives, and pollutants reshape population dynamics, evolutionary processes, and vulnerability to infectious disease using *Drosophila melanogaster*.

**Research strengths:** Population genetics · Host–pathogen interactions · Endogenous viral elements (EVEs) · Environmental toxicology · Behavioural genetics · Molecular biology

**Supervision:** 50+ undergraduate projects and 3+ postgraduate students

**Outreach:** National and international engagement in *Drosophila* education and biomedicine. Founder: Droso4Nigeria and BloSTEMEI

## Selected Achievements

1. **Built ABU's first Drosophila laboratory and Nigerian stock center**  
   Designed and established the lab from scratch. Enabled over 50 undergraduate and 3 postgraduate projects. Supported more than 10 manuscripts and successful funding bids including TETFund IBR and TWAS‑DFG.

2. **Established and strengthened Drosophila laboratories across Nigeria**  
   Established two labs and provided technical leadership that assisted in the establishment of three additional labs, creating a growing national network.

3. **Advanced ecotoxicology using Drosophila models**  
   Developed simplified behavioural and physiological assays for lead acetate and food additives. Generated in vivo evidence of toxicity mechanisms and mitigation strategies including resveratrol rescue. Published in *Environmental Toxicology and Pharmacology* (2024).

4. **Advanced endogenous viral element discovery in Drosophilidae**  
   Implemented a comparative genomics pipeline for EVE detection and curation, integrating population genetic context. Presented at BBSTEM Meeting (2025).

5. **Delivered Pan-African capacity building through Drosophila and STEM programmes**  
   Founded Droso4Nigeria and BloSTEMEI. Contributed to BioRTC and DrosAfrica as a trainer. Received Yobe State Governor's Award (2022).

6. **Contributed to high-impact collaborative genomics research**  
   Co-authored a *Genome Research* paper (2023) on mutation, recombination, and transposition rates in Drosophila.

7. **Advanced neuroscience research capacity in Africa**  
   Co-authored a study on simple model organisms for neuroscience research across Africa, published in *Molecular Psychology* (2024).

8. **Scaled teaching and postgraduate supervision in biology**  
   Taught nine modules to over 2,000 students. Supervised 50+ undergraduate and 3 postgraduate projects.

9. **Secured competitive research and capacity building funding**  
   Led and contributed to grants including NIF, SGCI TETFund, TWAS DFG, FlyBase, TReNDinAfrica, and DrosAfrica.

10. **Driving translational innovation through renewable energy solutions**  
    Co-invented AIRVOLT, a vertical axis wind turbine system for off-grid primary health centres. Patent reference: RP: NG/PT/NC/O/2025/18375

## Profile
Evolutionary geneticist and ecotoxicologist using Drosophila to study how environmental toxins shape population dynamics, evolution, and disease aetiology. Experienced in research, lab setup/management, and supervision. Strong commitment to science communication and equitable capacity‑building in Africa.

- Strengths: population genetics; host–microbe interactions; EVEs; environmental toxicology; behavioural genetics; molecular biology.  
- Supervision: 50+ undergraduate; 3+ postgraduate (official records).  
- Communication/outreach: extensive national and international engagement.

## Current appointments
- Postdoctoral Research Associate, Institute of Ecology and Evolution, University of Edinburgh, UK (Jan 2025 – present) — EVEs in Drosophilidae.  
- Research Scientist and Lecturer, Department of Zoology, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria (Mar 2019 – present).

## Education
- PhD Zoology, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria (Sept 2021).  
- MSc Biology, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria (Mar 2017).  
- PGDE Education, National Teachers Institute, Kaduna, Nigeria (Dec 2014).  
- BSc Biology, Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Nigeria (Nov 2012).

## Selected achievements
- Built ABU’s first Drosophila lab and Nigerian Drosophila stock center; enabled >50 UG and 3 PG projects; supported >10 manuscripts and multiple grants (TETFund IBR, TWAS‑DFG).  
- Established/mentored multiple Drosophila labs in Nigeria.  
- Advanced ecotoxicology with in vivo assays and mitigation (resveratrol; Environ. Toxicol. Pharmacol., 2024).  
- Initiated EVE comparative genomics pipeline (BBSTEM flash talk, 2025).  
- Capacity building via Droso4Nigeria, BloSTEMEI, BioRTC, DrosAfrica; Yobe State Governor’s Award (2022).  
- Co‑author, Genome Research (2023) on mutation, recombination, and transposition rates.  
- Patent co‑inventor: AIRVOLT wind turbine for off‑grid health centres (RP: NG/PT/NC/O/2025/18375).
